About Mohamed Sakr

Mohamed Sakr is the founder of Sakr Makes, a technology platform dedicated to turning engineering concepts into real products. As a Computer Engineering student and IEEE MET CSC Chairman, Mohamed Sakr leads initiatives that bridge academic theory with practical industry solutions.

Sakr Makes was founded by Mohamed Sakr to solve the gap between engineering education and product development. Through hands-on projects and strategic execution, Mohamed Sakr is building a platform where engineering meets reality.
