Sakr Makes

Mohamed Sakr — Founder of Sakr Makes

Sakr Makes by Mohamed Sakr is a creative maker brand exploring the intersection of digital design, 3D printing fabrication, and smart hardware systems. As the founder, I lead all research, mechanical design, and electronic integration.

  • Fusion 360 — Modeling functional structural enclosures and mechanical fittings.
  • 3D Printing — Operating hardware slicing controls, fine-tuning tolerances, and assemblies.
  • Prototyping — Wiring mechanical parts with controllers, battery modules, and sensor inputs.

IEEE MET CSC

Vice Chairman

IEEE MET CSC is the official IEEE Computer Society Chapter at MET Academy, Egypt. Together, we empower students through modern engineering methodologies and hands-on skill development.

  • Coordinating workflows and project timelines between tech and logistical committees.
  • Running workshops, interactive training bootcamps, and developer competitions.
  • Fostering peer-to-peer coding and AI learning circles.
  • Cultivating leadership, community organizing, and collaboration skills.
